How to use the map

Weathernaut provides a map courtesy of OpenStreetMaps and powered by Leaflet which will help you with looking for a location! Just click on map icon in the top navigation bar, and you may use the mouse to look around, zoom with the scrollwheel, or drop a marker on the map. If that's too much work for you, feel free to use the search bar located at the top of the map to help auto-complete to a new location!

How to use the Trip Planner

Looking for an efficient route under heavy weather, but don't know how to plan it out? Take a visit to our trip planner page at the top navigation bar! Enter an address as your destination, click on the type of transportation method you will be using, and check out the information we will give you!
